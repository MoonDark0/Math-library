mar:
Marching squares, pygame reccomended
Ymar(Size of screen(x),Size of screen(y),Range x(Middle of the screen is 0),Range y,Squares to march(Quality),Draw function(pygame.draw.line),Screen(pygame.display.set_mode((a,b))),Name of function to be evaluated)
Range example
Range x = 20
-10...0...10

Example:
screen=pygame.display.set_mode((1000,1000))
def Something(x,y):
	return x**3+2-567357
Ymar(1000,1000,20,20,50,pygame.draw.line,screen,Something)
